# EduTech-Task9-KPI-Analysis

## Tool Used
Power BI Desktop

## Dataset
Financial Sample Dataset (Kaggle)
Link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/atharvaarya25/financials

## KPIs Tracked
- Total Revenue (Gross Sales)
- Total Profit
- Total Units Sold

## Visualizations Created
- KPI Cards: Total Revenue, Total Profit, Total Units Sold
- Line Chart: Revenue trend by Month
- Bar Chart: Profit by Segment
- Pie Chart: Sales by Product
- Slicers: Country, Year, Segment

## Insights
- Government segment has highest profit
- Gross Sales peak observed in October-December (Q4)
- Paseo product has highest sales among all products

## Interview Answers
**What is a KPI?**
KPI (Key Performance Indicator) ek measurable value hai jo batata hai ki company apne business goals kitni effectively achieve kar rahi hai. Jaise Total Sales, Profit, Units Sold.

**How to track business health?**
Business health track karne ke liye KPIs monitor karte hain jaise Revenue, Profit margin, Units Sold. Power BI jaise tools se interactive dashboards banate hain jo automatically update hote hain jab data change hota hai.
